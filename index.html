About

Pari Ludin is a cultural worker, writer, musician, and singer based in Toronto. She holds a Bachelor of Arts from McMaster University and a Certificate in Arts Administration from Humber College. With over 5 years of experience in project management and arts facilitation, Pari has developed a unique approach to community-focused programs that bridge cultural divides.

As an Afghan-German-Canadian artist, Pari brings a rich cultural perspective to her work, creating spaces for dialogue and understanding through music, writing, and community engagement. Her practice centers on amplifying marginalized voices and fostering connections across diverse communities.
